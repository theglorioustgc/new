Devotional

Walking in Glorious Light

1 Peter 2:9 · Monday, July 4 2026

TOPIC: LIVE FOR MORE THAN THINGS Luke 12:15 KJV And he said unto them, Take heed, and beware of covetousness: for a man's life consisteth not in the abundance of the things which he possesseth.

It is God's desire for us as His children to have the best of things of life. He has blessed us with all things that is needed for life and godly living; he has done this so that we will never lack any good thing that we desire to have and therefore we can consistently enlarge and make progress.

This notwithstanding, it is important to realise that God wants us to have things, He doesn't want things to have us. Material things should not have a hold on you such that they determine your mood.

There are people who are happy or sad because of the things they possess or lack; they are happy when they have money and depressed when they don't seem to have money momentarily. Refuse to live a life of worry because of things. Do not let anything steal your joy.

While we desire more and pray for more, we must decide to be content with what we have now. The best way to train yourself to do this is by learning to give thanks for the things you have asked for in prayer. This also includes thanking the Lord intentionally for the things you have now. Thanksgiving is an application for more.

It is also important that you learn how to tie everything you own to the kingdom, this means find ways to use what you own for the advancement of the kingdom and also make commitment to use the things you are currently praying for to serve God. If the thing will not serve the kingdom, the Lord will not be committed to it.

Always remember there is more to life than material possessions.

Prayer Focus Father, I thank you for teaching me the power of contentment. Today, I have all things because you supply to me, I declare I am content with all I have. I live above materialism. Amen
