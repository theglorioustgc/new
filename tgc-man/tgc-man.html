The TGC Man

What It Means to Be a TGC Man

At The Glorious Church, we make men see the light of God's work until they become the full expression of his divine purpose and Glory. This is who the TGC Man is:

  1. He is Born again and filled with the Holy spirit with the evidence of speaking in other tongues.
  2. He is an anointed man and he functions by the ability of the Spirit of God and operates spiritual gifts.
  3. He has a personal relationship with God and observes personal times of fellowship with Him
  4. He is a Blessed and Favoured Man; He enjoys favour and acceptance everywhere he goes.
  5. He is a soul winner; He influences His world with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
  6. He is a man of Love; He walks in love effortlessly.
  7. He is a prosperous man; the grace of prosperity causes all his needs to be supplied; he is not stranded.
  8. As a husband; he is an exceptional husband and father; He is the priest of his Home.
  9. As a wife,she is a crown on the head of her husband; a true burden bearer and help meet for him.
  10. As a child, he is a son of consolation.
  11. He enjoys sound health and strength.
  12. He is at peace and his heart is full of joy.
  13. He excels in business and career; He is at the top of his field.
  14. In his world,he is respected and looked up to as a leader and inspiring figure.
  15. The TGC Man is the full expression of Jesus Christ everywhere; He walks in Dominion, Amen!
